<p><p>Raster encoders are precision devices used for position sensing in various applications. The market is primarily divided into two types: rotary encoders and straight edge encoders. Rotary encoders track angular position and are commonly found in motors, robotics, and industrial automation. Straight edge encoders, on the other hand, measure linear motion along a path, often used in CNC machines and manufacturing processes. Both types enhance accuracy and efficiency, making them essential in modern technological applications.</p></p>
