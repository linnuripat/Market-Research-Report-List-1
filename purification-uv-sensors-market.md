<p><p>The Purification UV Sensors market is segmented into three main types based on wavelength: UVA, UVB, and UVC. UVA sensors operate within the 320-400 nm range, mainly used in disinfection and sterilization applications. UVB sensors, covering 280-320 nm, are primarily focused on monitoring UV exposure and determining sun safety. UVC sensors, ranging from 100-280 nm, are critical for water and surface purification due to their effectiveness in inactivating microorganisms. Each type serves distinct purposes in various purification technologies.</p></p>
