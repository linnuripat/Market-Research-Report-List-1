<p><strong>The Pasture Mat Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thin</li><li>Thickened</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pasture Mat market can be categorized into two types: thin and thickened mats. Thin mats, typically less than 10 mm in thickness, are designed for lightweight use, providing basic support and moisture retention for grass growth. Thickened mats, on the other hand, exceed 10 mm in thickness and offer enhanced durability, improved drainage, and better insulation for roots. Both types serve specific agricultural needs, with thickened mats often preferred for areas requiring superior protection against soil erosion and compaction.</p></p>
