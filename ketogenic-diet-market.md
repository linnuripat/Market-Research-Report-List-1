<p><p>The Ketogenic Diet is a high-fat, low-carbohydrate dietary regimen designed to induce ketosis, a metabolic state where the body burns fat for fuel instead of carbohydrates. It has gained popularity for weight loss, improved energy levels, and potential therapeutic benefits for conditions like epilepsy and diabetes.</p><p>The Ketogenic Diet Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising health consciousness among consumers and a growing demand for effective weight management solutions.
