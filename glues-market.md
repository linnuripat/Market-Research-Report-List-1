<p><p>The glue market is categorized into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and others. Water-based glues are eco-friendly, easy to clean, and suitable for a variety of applications, often used in packaging and crafts. Solvent-based glues contain organic solvents, offering strong adhesion and durability, ideal for industrial applications but may pose health and environmental risks. The 'others' category encompasses specialty adhesives like hot-melt, epoxy, and polyurethane, catering to specific needs in sectors like automotive, construction, and electronics.</p></p>
