<p><p>The 3D Cone Beam Computed Tomography (CBCT) systems market is primarily segmented into two types: Image Intensifier Systems and Flat-Panel Imager Systems. Image Intensifier Systems utilize traditional technology to convert X-ray photons into visible light for imaging, offering high sensitivity but larger footprints. In contrast, Flat-Panel Imager Systems leverage digital detectors for enhanced image quality and smaller, more versatile designs. Both systems play critical roles in dental, maxillofacial, and orthopedic imaging, catering to diverse clinical needs.</p></p>
