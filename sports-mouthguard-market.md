<p><strong>The Sports Mouthguard Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Preformed Mouthguard</li><li>Thermoformed Mouthguard</li><li>Custom Mouthguard</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sports mouthguard market consists of three main types: preformed, thermoformed, and custom mouthguards. Preformed mouthguards are ready-to-wear and provide basic protection, ideal for casual athletes. Thermoformed mouthguards are made from thermoplastic materials that soften when heated and can be molded to the wearer’s teeth for a better fit. Custom mouthguards are individually crafted by dental professionals, offering the highest level of comfort and protection tailored to the athlete’s dental structure, making them ideal for serious sports participation.</p></p>
