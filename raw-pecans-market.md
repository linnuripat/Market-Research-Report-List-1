<p><p>The raw pecans market can be categorized into two main types: in-shell pecans and shelled pecans. In-shell pecans are sold whole, preserving their natural state and extending shelf life, making them popular for snacking and culinary uses. Shelled pecans, on the other hand, are processed for convenience, often used in baking, cooking, and as toppings. Each market type appeals to different consumer preferences, with in-shell options favored for freshness and shelled varieties preferred for ease of use.</p></p>
