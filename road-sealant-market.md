<p><p>The road sealant market is primarily categorized into polyurethane and polyester fiber types. Polyurethane sealants are known for their excellent durability, flexibility, and resistance to weathering, making them ideal for roads subjected to heavy traffic and harsh conditions. On the other hand, polyester fiber sealants incorporate fibers to enhance tensile strength and resistance to cracking, providing a tougher surface. Both types serve to extend the lifespan of roadways, protect against moisture infiltration, and improve overall pavement performance.</p></p>
