<p><p>The plastic decking market features various materials, each with unique properties. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is durable and resistant to moisture, making it ideal for outdoor use. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) offers flexibility and is often used in applications requiring lightweight materials. PP (Polypropylene) is known for its chemical resistance and toughness. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is popular for its cost-effectiveness and weather resistance. Other materials like PS (Polystyrene) and PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) are also utilized, providing diverse options for consumers.</p></p>
