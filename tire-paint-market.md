<p><strong>The Tire Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner Tire Paint (ITP)</li><li>Outer Tire Paint (OTP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tire Paint market is segmented into two primary types: Inner Tire Paint (ITP) and Outer Tire Paint (OTP). Inner Tire Paint is designed for the internal surfaces of tires, providing protection against moisture and corrosion, enhancing durability. Conversely, Outer Tire Paint is formulated for external application, focusing on aesthetics and surface protection from UV rays, weathering, and wear. Both types fulfill specific roles in enhancing tire longevity and appearance, catering to different consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
