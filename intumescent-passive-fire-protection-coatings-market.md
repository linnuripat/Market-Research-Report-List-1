<p><p>Intumescent passive fire protection coatings expand when exposed to heat, forming a char layer that insulates underlying materials. The market is divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based coatings offer low VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly and user-friendly. Solvent-based coatings provide stronger adhesion and enhanced durability but typically have higher VOC levels. Both types are essential for ensuring structural fire safety while catering to different application preferences and regulatory requirements.</p></p>
