<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Charging (EVC) market is divided into two primary types: on-board and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated into the vehicle, converting AC power from charging stations to DC power for the battery, facilitating convenient charging at home or work. Off-board chargers, on the other hand, are external units that provide high power output, often found in public charging stations, allowing for quicker charging times. Both types are essential to support the growing demand for electric vehicles.</p></p>
