<p><p>The Modified Bitumen Waterproof Membrane market is divided into two primary types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS membranes offer excellent elasticity and versatility, making them suitable for various climates and applications. They excel in low-temperature flexibility. In contrast, APP membranes are characterized by their strength and UV resistance, making them ideal for hot weather applications. Both types enhance durability and waterproofing efficiency in roofing and building applications, catering to different environmental conditions and performance needs.</p></p>
