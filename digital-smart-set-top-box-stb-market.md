<p><p>The Digital Smart Set-Top Box (STB) market encompasses three main types: Digital Cable, Terrestrial Digital, and Satellite Digital. Digital Cable STBs connect to cable networks, enabling users to access a variety of channels and on-demand content. Terrestrial Digital STBs receive signals from broadcast towers, providing free-to-air channels and services. Satellite Digital STBs access satellite signals, offering a wide range of programming, often including international channels. Each type caters to different viewing preferences and infrastructure, enhancing the entertainment experience.</p></p>
