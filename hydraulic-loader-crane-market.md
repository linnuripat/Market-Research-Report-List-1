<p><p>The hydraulic loader crane market includes two primary types: knuckle boom cranes and telescopic cranes. Knuckle boom cranes feature an articulated arm that allows for exceptional maneuverability, making them ideal for tight spaces and varied lifting angles. Telescopic cranes, on the other hand, use extending booms to reach greater heights and distances, providing versatility for a range of lifting tasks. Both types serve essential roles in construction, logistics, and other industries requiring efficient material handling solutions.</p></p>
