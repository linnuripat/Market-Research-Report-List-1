<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Marine Exhaust Gas Cleaning System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Closed Loop Scrubbers</li><li>Hybrid Scrubbers</li><li>Dry Scrubbers</li><li>Membrane Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine exhaust gas cleaning system market includes several types of scrubbers designed to reduce sulfur emissions from ships. Open loop scrubbers use seawater to neutralize exhaust gases, discharging treated water back to the ocean. Closed loop scrubbers operate with a closed water circuit, using a caustic solution for treatment and storing waste for disposal. Hybrid scrubbers can switch between open and closed modes. Dry scrubbers utilize solid absorbents, while membrane scrubbers employ selective permeation for gas separation and cleaning.</p></p>
