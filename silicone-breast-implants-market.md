<p><p>The silicone breast implants market is primarily categorized into two types: silicone gel implants and highly cohesive silicone implants. Silicone gel implants consist of a thick gel, providing a natural feel and appearance, often favored for their softness. Highly cohesive silicone implants, also known as "gummy bear" implants, use a denser gel that maintains its shape better and minimizes the risk of leakage.
