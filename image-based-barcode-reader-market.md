<p><p>The image-based barcode reader market is divided into two primary types: fixed-position scanners and handheld scanners. Fixed-position scanners are typically installed at checkout counters or conveyor systems, enabling automated scanning of items as they pass by, ensuring quick and efficient processing. In contrast, handheld scanners are portable devices used by employees for flexible scanning in various environments, such as warehouses or retail spaces, providing greater mobility and adaptability for inventory management and pricing processes.</p></p>
