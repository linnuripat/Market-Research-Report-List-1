<p><p>The removable lid boxes market encompasses two main types: hinged lid rigid boxes and lift-off lid rigid boxes. Hinged lid boxes feature a lid attached by a hinge, allowing easy access while ensuring secure closure. They are popular for gift packaging and premium products. Lift-off lid boxes, on the other hand, have a separate lid that completely removes from the base, offering a more open presentation. Both types enhance product visibility and appeal, making them suitable for retail and luxury items.</p></p>
