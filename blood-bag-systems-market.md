<p><ul><li>Single</li><li>Double</li><li>Triple</li><li>Quadruple</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blood bag systems are classified based on the number of compartments they possess, designed for different blood collection and storage needs. </p><p>- **Single** bags collect whole blood for immediate use. </p><p>- **Double** bags separate red blood cells and plasma, facilitating various transfusion options. </p><p>- **Triple** systems add a third compartment for platelets, enhancing component retrieval. </p><p>- **Quadruple** bags further include a compartment for cryoprecipitate. </p><p>- **Other** types may feature specialized compartments for research or specific medical applications, offering versatility in blood management.</p></p>
