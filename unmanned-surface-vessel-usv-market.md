<p><p>The Unmanned Surface Vessel (USV) market is categorized into three main types: Remote Control, Autonomous Control, and Semi-autonomous. Remote Control USVs are operated by human pilots from a distance, allowing for real-time maneuvering. Autonomous Control vessels operate independently using onboard sensors and software to navigate and perform tasks without human intervention. Semi-autonomous USVs combine both systems, enabling human operators to oversee operations while allowing the vessel to perform certain functions autonomously, enhancing efficiency and safety in various maritime applications.</p></p>
