Hetero-junction with Intrinsic Thin Layer (HIT) combines different materials for enhanced energy conversion. Back Contact Solar Cells eliminate front contact shading, optimizing light absorption. Passivated Emitter Solar Cells enhance surface passivation, reducing recombination losses.
