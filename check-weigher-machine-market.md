<p><strong>The Check Weigher Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>In-Motion Check Weigher Machine</li><li>Intermittent Check Weigher Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The check weigher machine market is primarily categorized into two types: in-motion check weigher machines and intermittent check weigher machines. In-motion check weighers operate continuously, allowing products to be weighed while moving along a conveyor, enhancing efficiency in high-speed production environments. In contrast, intermittent check weighers pause the product for weighing, making them suitable for lower-speed operations where precision is paramount. Both types ensure product quality and compliance by detecting weight variations in real-time.</p></p>
