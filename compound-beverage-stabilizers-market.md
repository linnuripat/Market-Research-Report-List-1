<p><p>The compound beverage stabilizers market comprises various types, including Gum Arabic, Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC), Xanthan Gum, and Carrageenan. Gum Arabic acts as a thickener and emulsifier, enhancing texture. CMC improves viscosity and shelf stability. Xanthan Gum provides thickening and suspending properties, often used in low-fat drinks. Carrageenan stabilizes and thickens dairy and non-dairy beverages. Other stabilizers may include guar gum and pectin, catering to diverse beverage formulations, optimizing mouthfeel, and ensuring product consistency.</p></p>
