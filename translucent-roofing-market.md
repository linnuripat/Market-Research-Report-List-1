PC sheets offer high impact resistance and are lightweight, suitable for both commercial and residential use. PET sheets are known for their clarity and UV resistance, while PMMA sheets provide excellent optical properties and are often used in decorative applications. PP sheets are cost-effective and versatile, suitable for various roofing needs.</p></p>
