<p><p>Vehicle Mounted Wheelchair Lifts are designed to enhance mobility for individuals with disabilities, and they come in three main types: Inside Lifts, Outside Lifts, and Hybrid Lifts. Outside Lifts are mounted externally, allowing for easy access without occupying interior space. Hybrid Lifts combine features from both types, offering versatility by allowing users to choose between internal and external access based on their needs.</p></p>
