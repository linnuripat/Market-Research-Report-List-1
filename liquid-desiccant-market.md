<p><p>The liquid desiccant market is categorized into two main types: organic solvents and inorganic solvents. Organic solvent desiccants, such as glycerol and certain alcohols, offer low toxicity and environmental benefits, making them suitable for various applications, including air conditioning and humidity control. In contrast, inorganic solvent desiccants, like calcium chloride and lithium bromide, provide efficient moisture absorption and are commonly used in industrial processes. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing energy efficiency and improving indoor air quality.</p></p>
