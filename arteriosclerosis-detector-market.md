<p><p>The arteriosclerosis detector market includes two primary types: pulse wave velocity detection and pulse waveform analyzer detection. Pulse wave velocity detection measures the speed of blood pressure waves traveling through arteries, providing insights into arterial stiffness, a key indicator of arteriosclerosis. Pulse waveform analyzer detection involves analyzing the shape and characteristics of the arterial pulse waveform to assess cardiovascular health and potential risk factors. Both methods contribute to early diagnosis and management of arteriosclerosis, improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
