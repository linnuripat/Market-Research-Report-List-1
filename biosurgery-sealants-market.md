<p><strong>The Biosurgery Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fibrin Sealant</li><li>Collagen Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biosurgery sealants market primarily comprises fibrin sealants and collagen sealants. Fibrin sealants, derived from human or animal blood, promote hemostasis and tissue adhesion in surgical procedures. Collagen sealants, made from natural collagen, enhance wound closure and tissue regeneration by providing structural support. Both types play crucial roles in reducing surgical complications, accelerating healing, and improving patient outcomes. Their applications span various medical fields, including orthopedics, cardiovascular surgery, and wound management, contributing to the growth of the biosurgery market.</p></p>
